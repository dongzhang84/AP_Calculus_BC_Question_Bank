Let \(R\) be the region in the first quadrant bounded by the graph of \(y = \sqrt{x - 1}\), the \(x\)-axis, and the vertical line \(x = 10\). Which of the following integrals gives the volume of the solid generated by revolving \(R\) about the \(y\)-axis?

(A) \(\pi \int_{1}^{10} (x - 1) \, dx\)
(B) \(\pi \int_{1}^{10} \left(100 - (x - 1)\right) \, dx\)
(C) \(\pi \int_{0}^{3} \left(10 - (y^2 + 1)\right)^2 \, dy\)
(D) \(\pi \int_{0}^{3} \left[100 - \left(y^2 + 1\right)^2\right] \, dy\)
