  1. \(f\) has a relative maximum on the interval \(0.4 < x < 2.4\).
  2. \(f\) has a relative minimum on the interval \(0.4 < x < 2.4\).
  3. The graph of \(f\) has two points of inflection on the interval \(0.4 < x < 2.4\).
(A) I only
(B) II only
(C) III only
(D) I and III only
(E) II and III only
