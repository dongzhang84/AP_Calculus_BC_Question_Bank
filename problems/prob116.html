Let \(f\) be the function with \(f(0) = \frac{1}{\pi^2}\), \(f(2) = \frac{1}{\pi^2}\), and derivative given by \(f'(x) = (x + 1)\cos(\pi x)\). How many values of \(x\) in the open interval (0, 2) satisfy the conclusion of the Mean Value Theorem for the function \(f\) on the closed interval [0, 2] ?

(A) None
(B) One
(C) Two
(D) More than two
